An Archaeological Exercise in Tree Age Estimation

2025.11.19

Oakland University exists on land once belonging to the Dodge estate. Prior to the university occupying this space, there were apple orchards planted around what is now Meadow Brook Rd., originally planted in the early 1900's and then having trees replaced as decades passed into the the 1960's.
I did some work finding out how old the remaining trees are which proved to be an interesting exercise in modeling tree growth with an archeological twist.
